Smart Kidz Club's Reading Circles are children's book clubs and will be led by teachers and authors with the goal for children to engage in reading books, fun activities, quizzes and conversations around related and relevant themes each week. In addition to building up knowledge, here's what your child will gain by the end of the session:

  Learn active participation as well as passive listening and observing
  Learn group interactions and dynamics amongst peers
  Learn to develop positive relationships with their peers
  Learn to communicate their feelings appropriately
  Come up with ways to collectively solve problems via age appropriate discussions around specific themes each week
  Self confidence and early exposure to public speaking

These virtual sessions will be conducted the same time every week, twice a week, for eight weeks. Parents can relax and let their child engage in a fun-filled and intellectually stimulating time with a fun and trusted adult instructor. First time registrants will receive a complimentary one year access to Smart Kidz Club Premium App, an $80 value, to continue their reading and learning throughout the year.

Bring a friend along to double the fun. Spots are limited, don't wait to sign up and reserve a spot.
